how many Classification in architecture Patterns?

Software Architecture Patterns are broadly divided into two classifications, Monolithic and Distributed

what is Monolithic?

single deployment unit or running in one application

what is Distributed?

multiple deployment units, usually consisting of several applications

what is benefit of Monolithic?

- easy to implement
- simple application
- easy to Distributed

what is benefit of Distributed?

- easy to maintain application
- consist of several unit 
- service running safetly without another service

when to use them?

- little team
- simple app 
- without business requirement

recommended: Monolithic Architecture

- large team
- complex app 
- include business requirement

recommended: Distributed Architecture
